Baby phone
You need a recent browser for this baby phone to work. It was tested on Firefox and Chrome and is designed for usual private home networks. So if you have a WiFi router and two devices (laptop, PC, tablet, phone, ...), both connected to the same WiFi - you are good to go. Video and audio is streamed directly between the two devices, so I don't get to see/hear your baby, just if you are concerned about privacy. You can change the name of your baby below, either for adding a personal touch or, if you have more than one, to tell them apart when connecting your parent device.
